Welcome to Offline Tippani ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

üìä 2. Data Collection

Offline Tippani is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your notes, content, and data remain exclusively on your device.

Here's what we DON'T do:

  • No user accounts or registration required

  • No tracking cookies or analytics
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No advertising or marketing data collection

üíæ 3. Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage:

  • Notes and content: Encrypted using SQLCipher (256-bit AES encryption)
  • Images and files: Stored in your device's secure app directory
  • Encryption keys: Securely managed using Android Keystore
  • No cloud sync: Data never leaves your device
  • Device-only access: Your data is only accessible through the app on your device

üîë 4. Permissions

  • Storage (Read/Write): To import documents (PDF, images) and export notes. Used only for file operations you initiate.
  • Internet (Optional): Only for accessing help documentation online. No data is transmitted.

These permissions are used solely for their stated purposes and do not involve data collection or transmission.
